# FinEdge Capital Pvt Ltd - Customer Onboarding & KYC Policy
**Version:** 1.0 (March 2026)
**Organization Type:** NBFC (Investment & Credit Company)

## 1. Objective
To establish a procedure for onboarding new customers and performing basic Know Your Customer (KYC) checks as per the management's baseline requirements.

## 2. Customer Identification Procedure
- For individual customers, we collect a copy of the Aadhaar card and verify identity via a one-time password (OTP).
- For corporate entities, we collect the Certificate of Incorporation and GSTIN.
- Verification of documents is completed within 14 business days of onboarding.

## 3. Risk Categorization
- Customers are categorized as 'Standard' or 'High Risk'.
- 'High Risk' customers require a senior manager's sign-off.

## 4. Record Maintenance
- All KYC documents and transaction records are maintained in our secure digital vault for a period of 3 years from the date of the transaction or closing of the account.

## 5. Ongoing Monitoring
- We perform a manual review of account transactions once every year to identify any suspicious activity.
- If suspicious activity is found, we notify the local police station.

---
**Note:** This is an internal draft and does not yet include integrations with central registries like CKYC or specialized AML monitoring software.
